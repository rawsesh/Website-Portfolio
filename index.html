About Me

I’m a social media marketer with multi-industry experience and a deep respect for the psychology behind consumer behavior. I don’t just create content—I study people. I pay attention to how backgrounds, lived experiences, and cultural shifts shape the way we engage with brands. Observation is my sharpest tool, and intuition—grounded in constant interaction with the world around me—often tells me what the data hasn’t yet. I thrive in fast-paced environments that evolve with the consumer, not despite them. My work is driven by the thrill of knowing something will land before it does—and the quiet pride that comes when it does. As someone from a generation often being marketed to, I know what earns trust, what deserves attention, and what makes people believe. And that’s what I bring into every strategy, every story, every scroll.
